About this project

Problem

Detect fraudulent card transactions in a dataset where fraud accounts for just 0.17% of records — a setting where naive accuracy is meaningless (99.8% accuracy is achievable by predicting "no fraud" every time).

Approach

Compared Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and XGBoost under class-weighted loss, benchmarked against an unsupervised Isolation Forest baseline. Selected XGBoost by PR‑AUC and tuned its decision threshold to balance precision and recall on the fraud class.

Stack

Python · Pandas · Scikit‑learn · XGBoost · Flask
